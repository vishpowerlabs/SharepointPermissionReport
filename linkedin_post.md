# LinkedIn Post Draft

**Headline:** 🔐 Take Control of Your SharePoint Permissions with a Single View!

**Post Content:**

Struggling to track "Who has access to what?" in your SharePoint sites? You're not alone.

Managing permissions in SharePoint Online can often feel like a guessing game involved checking individual lists and libraries manually. That’s why I built the **SharePoint Permission Viewer** web part.

🚀 **The Solution:**
A unified, single-pane-of-glass dashboard that visualizes all user and group permissions across your entire site collection.

✨ **Key Features:**
*   **Centralized Dashboard:** View Site, List, and Library permissions in one place.
*   **Visual Badges:** Instantly spot "Full Control" vs "Read" access with color-coded indicators.
*   **Deep Scan:** Drill down into any list to verify every single item for unique permissions.
*   **Smart Filtering:** Automatically hides "Limited Access" noise for a clean report.
*   **Theme Aware:** Automatically adapts to your site's look and feel.
*   **Exportable:** Download comprehensive reports to CSV for auditing.
